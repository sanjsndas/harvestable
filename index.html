    In‑depth tutorial: perfect pan sauce

    A proper pan sauce transforms a simple seared protein. Here is our framework for consistent, silky sauces every time.

  • Deglaze with wine or stock while the pan is hot
  • Scrape up all the fond (brown bits) — that’s flavour
  • Add aromatics: shallot, garlic, fresh herbs
  • Reduce by half, then mount with cold butter off the heat
  • Finish with a splash of vinegar or citrus for brightness

    FAQs

    Why does my sourdough not rise? Check starter activity and bulk fermentation temperature. We recommend 24‑26°C for optimal rise.
    Can I substitute whole wheat for AP? Yes, but adjust hydration: whole grains absorb 10‑15% more water.
    How to store fresh herbs? Treat them like flowers: stems in water, cover loosely with a plastic bag, refrigerate.
