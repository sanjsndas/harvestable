Privacy Policy

We harvest trust as carefully as we harvest ingredients. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Harvest Table Kitchen (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit our website, and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

Information we collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as when you:

  • Fill out a contact form (name, email, subject, message).
  • Subscribe to our newsletter or seasonal updates.
  • Comment on a recipe or story (if enabled).

We do not collect sensitive personal data unless you explicitly provide it. We do not sell or rent your information to third parties.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and fulfill requests.
  • To send you occasional seasonal recipes, kitchen tips, or harvest news — only if you’ve opted in.
  • To improve our website and content based on user feedback.

Cookies & tracking

We use minimal cookies to enhance your experience. These may include:

  • Essential cookies: for basic site functionality.
  • Analytics: we use a privacy-friendly analytics tool to understand which recipes are most popular.

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings. For more details, see our Cookies Policy.

Third-party services

We may use trusted third-party platforms (e.g., email newsletter providers) to help us communicate with you. These services are bound by their own privacy policies, but we choose partners who align with our values of transparency and data protection.

Data security

We implement reasonable physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to protect your information. However, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to keep your own information safe and to contact us if you suspect any breach.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Request a portable copy of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

Children’s privacy

Our site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised “last updated” date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

Questions? We’re just a message away.
